What's a Multimodal LLM?

A Multimodal LLM can model multiple modalities.
It's a neural network that combines sense data from multiple sources.
In LLM land, "multimodal" means models that process images + text.
A model with images + speech is called "multimodal".
Note that we're focusing on such a subfield, not just multitasking or fusion.

Let's contrast this to "fusion" where we mix inputs for one task.
Fusion is common in satellite remote sensing.
A multimodal LLM fuses to process images + text.

Captioning: Given an image, say what's happening in it.

This fits in content understanding.
It's not image generation.

What's the ImageNet Zero-Shot?

Most images have no labels.
Zero-shot is predicting classes not seen.
It's a chalenge for image recognition.
